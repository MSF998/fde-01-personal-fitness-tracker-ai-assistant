AI Recommendation

The guardrail state is the one that matters most for the demo — it must be visibly transparent, never a silent swap (Feature 5, AC4). The optional request field (AC5, added 2026-07-18) is what makes the guardrail demoable live — type an unsafe request and watch it get caught, rather than hoping the AI volunteers something unsafe on its own.
